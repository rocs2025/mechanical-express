FAQs

What is the main function of the engine in a car?

The engine is the fundamental part of a vehicle because it is responsible for generating energy to transform it into movement.

When should brake fluid be changed??

In ABS systems, the brake fluid must be changed every year or every 20,000 km, and in conventional systems, every 2 years or 40,000 km.

What is the body of a car?

The body is the external part of the car that covers the chassis and engine

When should spark plug wires be changed?

The spark plug wires must be changed every 4 years or every 80,000 km..
